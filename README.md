# Web Design & Branding Resources by Cider House Media

Cider House Media is a web design and digital marketing agency based in Easthampton, Massachusetts.  
We create story-driven websites and branding for small businesses, artisans, and community organizations across the United States.

This repository collects reference materials, design frameworks, and digital resources that support small business web design projects.

Visit [Cider House Media](https://ciderhouse.media "Web Design & Branding by Cider House Media") to learn more about our work.

## Included Resources

- **Small Business SEO Checklist** — Practical steps we use to optimize local websites.  
- **Web Design Best Practices** — Accessibility, usability, and mobile-first design guidelines.  
- **Storytelling Framework** — How narrative and brand voice shape online engagement.  
- **Creative Tools** — Design and writing assets that support small business branding.
